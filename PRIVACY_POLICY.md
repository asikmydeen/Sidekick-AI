# Privacy Policy for Dyad AI Chatbot

**Last Updated: December 5, 2024**

## Introduction

Dyad AI Chatbot ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Chrome extension.

## Summary

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to our servers.** All data stays on your device or is sent directly to the AI provider you choose to use.

---

## Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do **not** collect personal information
- We do **not** track your browsing activity
- We do **not** use analytics or tracking services
- We do **not** store your data on external servers
- We do **not** share any information with third parties
- We do **not** use cookies for tracking

---

## Data Storage

### Local Storage Only

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage APIs:

1. **API Keys** - Your API keys for AI providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.) are stored locally in Chrome's secure storage. They are never transmitted to any server except the AI provider you're communicating with.

2. **Chat History** - Your conversation history is stored locally on your device. You can delete it at any time through the extension interface.

3. **Settings & Preferences** - Your theme preferences, selected models, and other settings are stored locally.

---

## Third-Party AI Providers

When you use this extension, your messages are sent directly to the AI provider you select:

- **OpenAI** - [Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy)
- **Anthropic** - [Privacy Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy)
- **OpenRouter** - [Privacy Policy](https://openrouter.ai/privacy)
- **HuggingFace** - [Privacy Policy](https://huggingface.co/privacy)
- **AWS Bedrock** - [Privacy Policy](https://aws.amazon.com/privacy/)
- **Ollama** - Runs locally on your machine; no external data transmission

**We strongly recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any AI provider you choose to use.**

Your communications with these providers are subject to their respective privacy policies and terms of service. We have no control over how they process your data.

---

## Permissions Explained

The extension requests the following permissions:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| `storage` | Save your settings, API keys, and chat history locally |
| `activeTab` | Read page content when you choose to include it in your prompt |
| `scripting` | Enable the "Include Page Content" feature |
| `sidePanel` | Display the chat interface in Chrome's side panel |
| `declarativeNetRequest` | Handle API requests to AI providers |

---

## Page Content Access

The "Include Page Content" feature allows you to send the current webpage's text to the AI. This feature:

- Is **opt-in only** - you must explicitly enable it
- Sends content **directly to your chosen AI provider**
- Does **not** store or transmit page content to us

---

## Data Retention

- **Chat history**: Stored indefinitely until you delete it
- **API keys**: Stored until you remove them or uninstall the extension
- **Settings**: Stored until you reset them or uninstall the extension

To delete all data, simply uninstall the extension from Chrome.

## Security

- No sensitive data is logged or stored externally
